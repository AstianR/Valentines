Hibiscus + Amaryllis

Hibiscus burns like a heartbeat at sunrise, amaryllis rises like grace after rain. Both bloom different, both bloom fearless, and somehow your smile carries them the same. You are the color that softens every storm, the calm that still turns ordinary days bright. Like petals opening when love is near, you make everything around you feel right. If hibiscus is fire and amaryllis is light, then you are the garden where both belong. Beautiful in every flaw, every truth, every laugh, perfect to me, where my heart has been all along.
For you, my pretty lady.
